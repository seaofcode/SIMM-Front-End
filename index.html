Patient Education Portal

PEP Talks is solving the problem of patient education in Radiology & Medical Imaging. Our solution is focused on educating the patient prior to arrival in radiology. When patients are scheduled for X-rays, CTs, MRIs, and Ultrasounds, they may not know how to prepare themselves physically/mentally for the procedure.
